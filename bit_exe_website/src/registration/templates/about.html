About

"Revolutionizing Conveyor Reliability: Predictive Maintenance through Smart ML Solutions"

Problem Statement

Within our intricate conveyor systems, comprising over 18,000 poly pulleys, a persistent challenge arises due to the frequent wear and tear experienced by these crucial components. The core issue lies in the operational disruptions caused by the wear and tear, often leading to unplanned stoppages of the conveyor system. These unexpected halts force us into emergency replacements to resume normal functionality promptly. The repercussions extend beyond mere interruptions in our day-to-day operations, as the reactive approach to pulley failures incurs substantial costs. The financial burden associated with emergency replacements adds complexity to our operational landscape, prompting the need for a more proactive and sustainable solution.

Proposed Solution

Our solution involves deploying advanced machine learning algorithms that analyze past performance data, enabling us to foresee pulley wear and identify vulnerable points. This proactive strategy empowers us to schedule timely replacements, preventing unexpected downtime and optimizing the overall efficiency of the conveyor system.
